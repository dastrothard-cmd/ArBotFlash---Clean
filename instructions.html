How to use it

Study, filter, reveal, repeat.

A simple guide for using the current ArbotFlash deck while the full games, accounts and duelling systems are being built.

1. Build a deck

Use search and filters to narrow the cards by family, region, form or identifying words such as bark, sheoak, cones or marri.

2. Study mode

Study mode shows the specimen name immediately. Use it when learning new species or checking field features.

3. Quick quiz

Quick quiz hides the ID. Look at the photo and feature cards first, then hit Reveal ID.

4. Voice controls

Use Speak card to read the current card aloud. Use Stop to shut it up. Pick the best available English/Australian voice from the dropdown.

5. Skins

Use Forest, Night or Field book skin from the main deck page. Skins are visual only and do not change your deck.

6. Photo slots

Each card is being prepared for multiple photos: whole form, bark/trunk, leaves, flowers, fruit, cones and diagnostic features.

Current build note

This version is a product-shell stage. The main job is to make the 80 current specimen cards beautiful, useful and game-ready before scaling toward the long eight-kingdom Catalogue-of-Life system.
